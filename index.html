About

Dear Respected Colleagues, It is with great pleasure that we invite you to participate in the International Conference on Materials, Waste Treatments, and Sustainable Energy (ICMWSE 2024), which will take place on May 9-10, 2024.

  • The ICMWSE 2024 conference will be hybrid (in-person and online).
  • French and English are the official presenting languages of ICMWSE 2024.
  • The book summary and the book in full text (current book) will be published on the national library's website following the conclusion of the conference.

  • The conference will be held from 9 to 10 May,2024.
